Three Body Problem Simulation

Inspired by the Trisolaran System from the Rememberance of Earth's Past Trilogy by Cixin Liu

The simulation below depicts a solar system with three stars orbiting each other and the chaotic nature of three bodies influencing each other. Unlike the two-body problem which has been solved (we have an equation which can perfectly predict the system's state at any point) the three body problem is unsolvable - for now at least. After reading the Three Body Problem, I was intrigued with this idea wanted to simulate it.

The Green, Blue & Red bodies are stars, and the yellow body is representative of the planet Trisolaris.
